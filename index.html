About Jonathan

The Man Behind the Role

Jonathan Roumie (born July 1, 1974, New York City) is an American actor, voice artist, and public speaker best known worldwide for his transformative portrayal of Jesus of Nazareth in The Chosen — the most-watched Christian media series in history.

After years of struggle in Los Angeles, Roumie surrendered his career to God, and three months later, The Chosen came along. His portrayal has since been seen by hundreds of millions of viewers in over 180 countries.

In 2023, he portrayed charismatic hippie evangelist Lonnie Frisbee in the film Jesus Revolution. He is also a dedicated Catholic, a keynote speaker at the March for Life, and a narrator for the Hallow prayer app.

Roumie is an illustrator, voice actor (Fallout 4, Death Stranding 2), and co-producer of live passion performances. He hosts live prayers on Instagram, some viewed nearly 100,000 times.
