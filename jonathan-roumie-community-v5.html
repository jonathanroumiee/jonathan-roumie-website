Jonathan Roumie is an American actor, voice artist, and public speaker best known worldwide for his portrayal of Jesus of Nazareth in The Chosen ‚Äî the most-watched independent streaming series in history. A first-generation American, his father was of Syro-Lebanese descent raised in Cairo, Egypt, and his mother grew up in Ireland.

Roumie studied film at the School of Visual Arts in New York, and after graduating, he briefly played drums in a contemporary rock band before moving to Los Angeles to pursue acting. He worked early in his career as a location scout on major productions including Spider-Man, National Treasure, and I Am Legend.

His path to The Chosen was not without struggle. After eight years in Los Angeles, Roumie was nearly broke and relying on government assistance when he met creator Dallas Jenkins in 2014. Three months after surrendering his career to God, Jenkins cast him as Jesus ‚Äî a role that would change his life and millions of others forever.

Beyond acting, Jonathan is a passionate Catholic advocate, public speaker, narrator for the Hallow prayer app, and a keynote speaker at events including the March for Life and the National Eucharistic Congress 2024. In 2024, he received an honorary doctorate in fine arts from the Catholic University of America.
